What is Scabies?

Scabies is a contagious skin condition caused by tiny mites that burrow into the skin, leading to intense itching and rash. It spreads through close contact with an infected person.

Causes of Scabies

Scabies is primarily spread through:

  • Skin Contact: Prolonged physical contact with an infected person.
  • Shared Items: Using infested bedding, towels, or clothing.
  • Overcrowded Areas: High-risk environments like nursing homes and dormitories.

Precautions and Tips

To prevent scabies, consider these tips:

  • Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  • Wash clothes, bedding, and towels frequently.
  • Vacuum your home regularly to remove mites.
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist.
