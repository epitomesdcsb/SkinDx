MAJOR SKIN PROBLEMS

Acne

A skin condition that occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells.

Common Treatments: Topical creams, antibiotics, and lifestyle changes.


Eczema

A condition that makes your skin red and itchy. It's common in children but can occur at any age.

Common Treatments: Moisturizers, steroid creams, and antihistamines.


Ringworm

A fungal infection that causes a ring-shaped rash on the skin.

Common Treatments: Antifungal creams, oral medications, and keeping the area dry.


Scabies

A contagious skin condition caused by tiny mites that burrow into the skin.

Common Treatments: Prescription creams and oral medications.
