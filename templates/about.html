📊 About the Project

🩺 Our Skin Disease Detection project is designed to help users identify potential skin conditions effortlessly. 📸 By scanning an uploaded image, it uses advanced algorithms to detect diseases with high accuracy. 🤖 A built-in chatbot assists users in navigating the platform and provides step-by-step guidance. 🧍 Users can also select age, gender, and body part from the disease selector to view possible conditions and their details. This intuitive platform empowers users with fast and reliable health insights for better decision-making! 🌟
