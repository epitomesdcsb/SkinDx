Complete Eczema Care Guide

What is Eczema?

Eczema is a skin condition that causes inflammation, redness, dryness, and itching. It can be triggered by allergens, irritants, stress, or environmental factors.

Causes of Eczema

The most common causes of eczema include:

  • Genetics: A family history of eczema increases the risk.
  • Allergens: Exposure to pollen, dust, or pet dander can trigger symptoms.
  • Climate: Dry or cold weather can worsen eczema.
  • Stress: Emotional stress can contribute to flare-ups.

Precautions and Tips

To keep eczema under control, consider these tips:

  • Use mild, fragrance-free skincare products.
  • Wear soft, breathable fabrics to minimize irritation.
  • Keep skin hydrated to prevent flare-ups.
  • Consult a dermatologist if symptoms persist.
